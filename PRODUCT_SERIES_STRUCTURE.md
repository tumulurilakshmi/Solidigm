## Validation Notes

For validating product series pages, check for:
1. **Series Title** - "SSD D3 Series", "D5 Series SSDs", "SSD D7 Series"
2. **Product Cards** - Each product should have:
   - Product name/ID
   - Interface type
   - Form factor
   - Capacity range
   - "View Details" link
   - "Compare" button
3. **Filter Options** - Interface, Form Factor, Capacity filters
4. **Related Articles** - Section at bottom
5. **Product Comparison** - Maximum 5 products can be selected
